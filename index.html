Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about building your dream home with us

How long does it take to design a house naksaa in Nepal?

A standard residential naksaa (blueprint) typically takes 7–15 working days depending on complexity. This includes initial consultation, preliminary sketches, revisions, and the final municipality-ready drawings. Urgent designs can be expedited upon request.

What is the typical cost per square foot for house construction in Kathmandu?

Construction costs in Kathmandu typically range from NPR 2,500 – 4,500 per square foot depending on finish quality, materials, and design complexity. We provide a detailed, transparent cost breakdown before any work begins.

Do you provide services outside of Kathmandu Valley?

Yes! Pasa Real Estate provides services across all of Nepal — from Kathmandu Valley to Pokhara, Chitwan, Biratnagar, Butwal, and beyond. Our team travels to client sites for consultations and supervision.

How long does it take to build a standard 2–3 storey house?

A typical 2–3 storey residential house takes 12–24 months from foundation to handover, depending on size and design complexity. We provide a detailed project timeline before construction and have a strong track record of on-time delivery.

Is Pasa Real Estate a government-registered company?

Yes, absolutely. Pasa Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. is officially registered with the Government of Nepal's Ministry of Industry, Commerce and Supplies (Reg. No: 393726/82/83, incorporated June 2, 2026) and holds a valid PAN (623630819).

Can I get a 3D design before committing to full construction?

Absolutely! We offer standalone 3D architectural design services without requiring a full construction contract. You can commission the 3D renders to visualize and approve your home design, then decide on construction.

Do you help with municipality approval and building permits?

Yes! Our team handles complete municipality documentation and approval assistance. We prepare all required drawings, structural reports, and documents as per municipality standards. We have experience with approvals across Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur, and municipalities across Nepal.
